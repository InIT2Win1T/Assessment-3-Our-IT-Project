Aims

 

Aim - Develop a fully functioning website to track fantasy football statistics

 

The aim of our IT project is to develop a website and mobile app that tracks and displays AFL statistics for AFL Fantasy players. Both applications will feature two versions, one being a free version and the other, a paid version. Though for this assignment we have chosen to prioritize the website over the app for two main reasons - simplicity to design, and to reach the maximum number of potential users as even if a user would like to access the tool from their mobile phone, they can just use the web application from their phone. We aim to fill a gap in the market for AFL fantasy statistics that exists currently with our website and are hoping to provide users with the most useful, relevant and up to date statistics for AFL Fantasy. And to display them in the best way possible to help users make much more informed decisions about the players they choose, than on any other platform and achieve a “one stop shop” of all statistics and help tools to establish a healthy user base to market our premium version to. To achieve this aim, we at InIT2winIT are going to have to meet three main goals - to scrape the data of the web, to create the website, and to develop an audience.

 

Goal - Develop a system to scrape the statistics and present them to users

 

While developing the website for our users we also must gather the data from a source of statistics such as footywire.com or the official AFL website and perform a “web scrape” from the web into a spreadsheet using a software called Apify, where we store all of our statistics on every single player in the AFL. Statistics gathered will include AFL fantasy price, kicks, marks, handballs, tackles, free kicks for and against and many more. These will be presented to the user with a system we’ve developed called player cards, where each AFL player has their own ‘card’ that displays all the relevant statistics about them and their recent matches. This is our most important goal to achieve, as this is the core of our service, and there is no use having the website without having the statistics.

 

Goal - Develop the website

 

To achieve our aim of having a fully functioning website, first we have to build the website and all the features that must be included. These minimum features to be implemented are a login portal for our users to be able log into their accounts, a way to view all the statistics we've gathered and compare players. We need a login portal and accounts system to distinguish between users using our premium service that will have extra features like being able to save your fantasy team and to receive recommendations on players. The premium version will be our main source of revenue. Otherwise, we will offer a free service where we will show advertising banners. To get our website working we must have it hosted through a cloud-based hosting service like Amazon Web Services as there are also cloud based servers for us to store our statistics database on with Amazon Web Services. It is vital that we achieve this goal if we are to achieve our overall aim as we need a way for users to be able to see and interact with the statistics that we gather.

 

Goal - Develop an audience

 

Finally, one of our goals is to create awareness of our website and develop a user base of premium customers. To do this we have to market our service to the right audience, by running advertisements on websites that relate to our topic, like footywire.com and news sites like Fox Sports on articles about AFL. As Well as advertising on Facebook, Instagram, Reddit and YouTube. While being less important in regards to development this is still a very important goal as without users our website cannot succeed and cannot continue to be supported.
