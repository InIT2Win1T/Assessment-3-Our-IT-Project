Graphic Designer

 

Job Description

 

As a quality graphic designer, you are to collaborate with web developer in delivering visually stunning, catchy website and app content. You are someone who is exceptional at conveying messages across to target audience graphically and can yield visual elements such as space, texture, colour, form and shape purposefully.

 

Technical

 

  • Graphic design skill with Adobe Creative Suite such as Photoshop, Dreamweaver & Illustrator

  • Capable of tweaking and manipulating images

  • Excels in infographics and can create 3D artefacts

  • Knowledge of web design

  • Strong grasp of HTML and CSS

  • Strong grasp of web design CMS platform WordPress

  • Competent in what, where, when and how to apply typography in various layouts and colour schemes

  • Willing to learn and master more graphic design software such as Quark when it is needed

 

Soft Skills

 

  • A team player

  • An individual with time management skill

  • Someone who represents ideas via aesthetic sketching/artwork

  • A confident and articulate presenter

  • A creative and artistic thinker

  • An individual who is attentive to minor details

  • An individual who is able to take in constructive criticism regarding his/her artwork

 

Qualifications

 

  • A Bachelor’s degree in graphic design or

  • A Bachelor’s degree in visual communication or

  • A Bachelor’s degree in related fields or

  • A Postgraduate degree in any of abovementioned field or

  • No degree with at least 3 or more years of industry experience as a graphic designer

 

Experiences

 

  • 3 or more years of industry experience using Adobe Creative Suite

  • Experienced in working with a diverse media with Adobe Creative Suite

  • Has experience in working with multiple projects under tight schedule
